January 7, 2026

Hot Mic Messaging System

Feature

We've added Hot Mic, a new way to communicate with your team in real-time. Send quick messages, images, and stay connected while you work.

The Hot Mic toggle lives right in your tab bar - click the fire emoji to go live. When Hot Mic is on, your team can see you're available and message you directly.

  • Real-time messaging with team members
  • Share images and screenshots instantly
  • Visual indicator when Hot Mic is active
  • Unread message notifications with blue dots
January 7, 2026

Data Policy Notices

Feature

We believe in transparency about where your data lives. You'll now see clear indicators in the footer showing whether your data is stored locally, shared with your team, or sent to Field Theory.

Different views have different policies - your clipboard history stays on your machine, while shared fields are synced with your team.

January 7, 2026

Auto-Updater Improvements

Fix

Fixed an issue where users on pre-release versions couldn't automatically update to stable releases. The update button now has proper visual feedback when clicked.

We also added a release notes popup that appears after updates, so you'll always know what's new.

January 6, 2026

Improved Tab Navigation

Improvement

Tab now cycles through Fields, Shared Fields, and Hot Mic in a loop. Shift+Tab goes backwards. No more dead ends.

We also updated the Commands icon to use a forward slash, matching the keyboard shortcut convention.

January 5, 2026

Authentication Required for Social Features

Feature

Hot Mic and Feedback now require you to be signed in. If you're not authenticated, you'll see a sign-in prompt instead of the feature.

This ensures your messages are properly attributed and your conversations are secure.
