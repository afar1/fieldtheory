## Engagement Principles

1. **Value first, always.** Answer the person's actual question before mentioning Field Theory.
2. **Be technical.** HN and r/LocalLLaMA respect depth. Share specifics about whisper.cpp, latency, audio handling.
3. **Acknowledge competitors honestly.** "Wispr Flow is great for general dictation. We focused specifically on the AI coding workflow."
4. **Don't post the same thing everywhere.** One subreddit per day max. Vary the angle.
5. **Build karma first.** Engage genuinely in target communities for a week before posting about your product.
6. **Share the problem, not the solution.** "I found that voice captures more context than typing for AI prompts" > "Try my app."
