Priority Mic

Designate your preferred microphone and Field Theory will always use it, even when macOS switches defaults.

Plug in a USB mic or connect AirPods and macOS changes your default input. Field Theory ignores that. Your priority mic stays locked until you say otherwise.

Verbal commands and transcription

Try saying "use the commit command" while transcribing anything. Field Theory will recognize and invoke any command in your directory.

Voice commands feel natural because they don't require you to change mediums to ensure a model runs any given sequence or macro. "Run [____] command" and the Field Theory will know and insert.

Auto-improved context

Think outloud and Field Theory will gently tidy long-form prompts and associate screenshots and commands with your prompt.
